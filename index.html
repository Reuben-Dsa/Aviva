About Aviva

Founded and led by proprietor - Godfrey Alex Lobo, Aviva Caretaker Training Centre is a dedicated educational hub based in Vakola, Mumbai, specializing in caregiver and caretaker training services. We are recognized under the Educational Support Services industry and are committed to delivering comprehensive, practical, and compassionate training programs. Our mission is to equip aspiring caretakers with the skills, confidence, and accreditation needed to excel in caregiving professions. At Aviva, we combine certified curriculum, hands-on learning, and experienced instructors to deliver high-standard training. From foundational caregiving techniques to advanced elder-care practices, our curriculum are structured to meet industry requirements and community needs. Situated in the heart of Mumbai, our centre offers modern facilities and an engaging learning environment that fosters both personal and professional growth.

Why Choose Aviva?

  • ✔ Accredited & Recognized Curriculum: Our certified program meets national standards, ensuring qualifications are trusted by employers and pave the way for further professional development .
  • ✔ Hands-On, Real-World Training: We prioritize immersive learning through practical labs, simulations, and real-case roleplays — giving you the confidence to apply skills directly in caregiving settings.
  • ✔ Expert Instructors & Mentorship: Our team of experienced caregivers and trainers offer both theoretical depth and personalized support, nurturing competence and compassion in every learner
  • ✔ Focus on Safety & Quality Care: Comprehensive instruction in safe handling, medication management, and environment setup reduces risks and improves outcomes for those under your care .
  • ✔ Empathy-Driven, Person-Centred Approach: We emphasize emotional intelligence, effective communication, and dignityfocused practices — ensuring care that respects each individual’s unique needs .
